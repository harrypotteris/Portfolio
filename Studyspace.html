Study Space

I built Study Space as a focused and interactive web interface designed to help users stay productive while studying or working. It simulates a calm digital environment with useful tools and seamless media integration.

The interface features a minimalist and responsive layout that keeps distractions at bay. I added an animated light bulb with a smooth switch interaction to recreate a cozy real-world study vibe. To enhance focus, I embedded a Spotify playlist for relaxing background music and included a looping study video to simulate a quiet, working atmosphere.

A live IST clock keeps users aware of the time, while a built-in timer with start, pause, and reset functions helps track study sessions and maintain flow.
